Jme Shop

We've collaborated with a group of truly talented designers and artisans to create a collection of well thought-out pieces that bring effortless style to good times at home. Weve got 9 brand new products in our Autumn Winter range including great Christmas gift ideas and stocking fillers for friends and family.

 
JAMIE AT HOME SHOP

Jme Food

Sadly you can't buy our gorgeous selection of Jme food through the online shop, but why not pop into a Recipease store, which is packed to the rafters with our products!

Visit JME food

Fifteen shop

Jamie's Fifteen gives young unemployed people a chance at a new life through food, while serving up some seriously delicious Italian food to diners. If you can't make it to the restaurant, give a gift that gives twice by buying some beautiful (and educational!) Fifteen kitchenware and edible gifts for a loved one, and help fund Fifteen for future generations.

Visit the fifteen shop

Jamie Oliver Food & Home products

Jamie's passion for fresh, honest and delicious food forms the basis for his brilliant range of food and kitchenware products, which ranges from oils and vinegars to kitchen spices, and gadgets to must-have tools. Find stockists, nutritional information and recipes here.

Visit jamie oliver products

Wood Fired Ovens

Jamie fell in love with wood-fired ovens while working with one at the River Caf茅. He loved the smoky flavours and personality they give to food, so he teamed up with Valoriani, a century-old Italian company that builds beautiful wood-fired ovens, to offer them to food lovers all over the UK.

Visit wood fired ovens

Jamie Magazine

What better gift for a foodie than a year's subscription to Jamie Magazine? A year's worth of recipes, fascinating articles and beautiful design delivered to your door 10 times a year.

Visit jamie magazine

Jamie's Italian shop

Get a bit of Jamie's Italian at home! We've got a selection of our beautiful terracotta roasters, antipasti planks and tableware for sale in our online shop. On top of that we've got lots of great gifts and tasty treats on offer too. Come in and have a look.

Visit Jamie's Italian shop
